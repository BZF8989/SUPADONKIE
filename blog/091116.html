Luxury & Supercar Weekend - 09.11.2016

With Vancouver being home to so many luxury and super cars I hardly bat an eye when I see a brand new Lamborghini or Porsche anymore. Luxury Supercar Weekend had to offer was beyond what we see everyday in Richmond. When I saw Singer Vehicle Design's Porsches on the list my $50 was out the door.

I arrived nice and early to beat the crowd since first day open to public. I had other appointments booked in the afternoon that day, which meant I had just a little over two hours to check out as much as I could. That explains why I didn't get any shots of the Pagani and the GT NSX D:

Anyway, enough yapping. Enjoy the pics, and share if you like what you see. ;)
